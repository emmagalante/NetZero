How to Help

Consider renewable energy sources. Regularly heating and cooling your home adds roughly 1.5 tons of CO2-equivalent to your annual footprint.

Consider if the travel is essential. Car and planes are the main culprits of CO2 emissions. Regularly driving a car adds an average of 2.4 tons of CO2-equivalent to your yearly footprint, while just a single transatlantic flight adds 1.6 tons.

Consider drying your clothes outside instead of dryers. Everday your laundry adds 0.25 tons of CO2 when heating the water and another 0.21 tons from drying your clothes.”
